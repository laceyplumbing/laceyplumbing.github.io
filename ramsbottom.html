Taking Care of Your Central Heating

As a crucial aspect of your home, it is important to take care of your central heating system. In more recent years these have become lighter, more economical and in some cases easier to afford. However, without proper care and maintenance problems can still develop.

One of the most common issues to come across is corrosion within the pipework. As water circulates around the metal components, it is easy for a natural reaction to happen. Unfortunately this can leave metal to enter the system and form what we know as radiator sludge. Leaving this to fester can cause issues with water supply and also impact the efficiency of your radiators.

Other things to look out for include limescale as well as household dirt within the systems. If there is something wrong with your system, then there are a few signs to be aware of. Radiators that are noisy, take a long time to heat up, or have mixed cold and hot patches could have problems that need resolving.

Installing a New Boiler

When a boiler reaches 10 years it is deemed to be inefficient. To add to this, an old system could be costing you more money than it’s worth. New boiler installations in Ramsbottom are easy to do, saving you time and money in the long run. There are many different types of boiler to choose from, giving you plenty of choice.

Condensing boilers are being used more often as these are extremely efficient and environmentally friendly. It is estimated that they can improve efficiency by around 30% by converting 90% of gas to heat. As well as seeing a decline in the price you pay, your home and central heating system will be more effective.
