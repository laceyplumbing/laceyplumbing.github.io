A new boiler, installed by a team you can trust.

Here at Lacey Plumbing & Heating we don’t just install new boilers. Behind every job we do there is a team of family friendly people working away to make sure you receive the best service possible on all your plumbing and heating work carried out by our engineers.

So who are the people behind Lacey Plumbing & Heating? Well you can see our team picture here. It’s not our best photo! But we’re human and it was taken at a team health and safety meeting. We are all about being human, treating others as we expect to be treated and that’s how we work when we are in your home. Our values are that simple. If you’re spending your hard-earned money, like us, we expect it to be spent on products and services that will last. We want you to be happy and our customers for life.

So whether you need a new boiler, boiler service, have a leaking tap or need a complete new bathroom you can be confident that we not only have the skills to do this work but you are also going to get a great all round service.  Afterall it’s nice to be nice and we love happy customers.

We look forward to working with you.
